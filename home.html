On the morning he must travel for his dead end job, Gregor Samsa wakes up to find himself transformed into a giant insect. Despite this, he knows he must catch the train for work so he can support his family.
Gregor glumly thinks about his life, and reflects on how sad and tired he is and how he wishes to quit his dead-end job but can’t for the sake of his family.
His family begins to worry and are knocking on the door. He tries to answer but the words won’t come out properly.
Gregor gets the door open and his family is immediately afraid of him. He tries to explain but they do not listen or look at him.
His father chases him back to his room. He scrapes his side on the door frame causing him to become severely injured and he goes into a deep sleep.
